About Pathfinders

Pathfinders was founded in 1977 by John Murphy with a staff of four. Today the company remains privately owned but has a staff of over 1000 and has made travel arrangements for over 16 million clients.
Experts in tailormaking itineraries worldwide, Trailfinders offers unbeatable value and exceptional service. 47 travel centres are staffed by the most experienced travel consultants in the industry who are on hand 7 days a week, located throughout the UK and Ireland. With a worldwide reputation, we have the widest range of flights, tours, hotels, cruises, car & motorhome hire across the globe. From budget to luxury our prices are amongst the lowest you will find anywhere.
